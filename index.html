Why Tow Truck Insurance Is Essential In Australia

Tow truck business insurance isn't just smart business practice - it's often legally required across Australian states.

Comprehensive Truck Coverage

Full protection against collision, theft, fire, storm damage, vandalism, and more. Affordable premiums ensure your business stays covered, with repair costs and replacement vehicles included.

Customer Vehicle Protection

On-hook liability insurance safeguards vehicles being towed using hooks, tilt-tray, or jib and boom mechanisms. It covers the repair or replacement costs if a customer’s vehicle not owned by you gets damaged while in transit for collection or delivery, within a 200-metre radius of the tow truck.

Business Continuity

Business interruption insurance helps your business recover from unexpected losses and continue normal operations without major setbacks.

Marine Cargo Insurance

Items not insured under hook liability, like machinery on the tray, are covered Australia-wide under this policy. It protects against loss, theft, or damage during transit, with cover limits of $100,000, $250,000, or $500,000.

Public Liability Insurance

This insurance covers you against legal liability to pay compensation if a third party suffers injury, loss, or property damage due to your business services or activities.
